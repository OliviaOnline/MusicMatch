Tuba Techniques & Valve Position Finder

Playing the Tuba

Blow through the mouthpiece with an "o" shape using your lips. Press the mouthpiece firmly against your lips, inhale deeply, and blow out. Keep the corners of your lips tight and the center loose to produce a good tone. Avoid puffing your cheeks as it may cause a rough sound.

Playing Higher and Lower Notes

For higher notes, tighten your lips and increase the air speed. For lower notes, relax your lips and maintain a steady airflow. Ensure the air remains steady and avoid unnecessary pressure on the mouthpiece.
